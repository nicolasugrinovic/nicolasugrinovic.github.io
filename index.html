Nicolas Ugrinovic

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Carnegie Mellon University, where I work with Kris Kitani. Previously, I worked at Meta (FAIR) as a Postdoctoral Researcher.

I obtained my Ph.D. from IRI under the supervision of Francesc Moreno-Noguer and Alberto Sanfeliu. During this time, I interned at Amazon Science with Alex Vorobiov. I was also a visitor at Stanford University in Leonidas Guibas Lab and at Naver Labs Europe with Gregory Rogez. I also worked in Leonidas Guibas Lab at Stanford University as a visiting researcher.

My research is in areas of computer vision and machine learning, specifically on 3D human pose/motion estimation, human motion generation, and reconstruction of 3D bodies.
